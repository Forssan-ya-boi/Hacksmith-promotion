The Hacksmith is a group of creative people who take cool things out of movies(usually marvel) and make it real. Some examples are, the thor hammer, the captain america shield, Iron mans mini jet packs on the arms and legs, etc. On top of this they also come up with crazy ideas where they build things like a mini cybertruck, 50 cal electric coil gun
